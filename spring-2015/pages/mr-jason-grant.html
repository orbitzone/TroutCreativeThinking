Jason's
Tips

  • Day Spa is about creating your own personal wellness oasis. A place where you can escape the outside world.
  • Favour organically inspired designs to introduce a sense of calm and serenity.
  • Take inspiration from water. Recreate a waterfall like experience with a floor mounted bath mixer or rain shower.
  • Bring the outdoors in with plenty of light. A big garden window or skylight make a great addition.
  • Beautiful framed photography and candles are the perfect way to finish off the bathroom and make the space feel inviting.

Jason's
Tips

  • A blend of modern and traditional influences, Neo Classic brings a real sense romance to the bathroom.
  • Go for a bold, monochromatic colour scheme that juxtaposes light and dark, for a dramatic high impact look.
  • Use classically inspired fixtures as showpieces to enhance the theatre of the room.
  • High-end surfaces and finishes will further enhance the sophistication and elegance of the space.

Tips

  • Coastal living is about celebrating the simply joys of life. Go for clean, contemporary products balanced with little art pieces to inject personality into the space.
  • You can layer textures, tones and patterns to create a sense of warmth. The addition of natural timber such as a wooden stool will also bring the space to life. Plants will make the space feel fresh and alive.
  • Elevate the experience and make it really special with the addition of luxury touches like gorgeous designer towels.
