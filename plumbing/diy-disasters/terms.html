2013 DIY Plumbing Disasters Competition - Terms and Conditions

  1. Information on how to enter and prizes form part of the conditions of entry. Entry into the 2013 DIY Plumbing Disasters Competition shall be deemed an acknowledgement and acceptance of these terms and conditions.
  2. Entry to the Competition is free and only open to Australian residents who are Reece Account Customers during the period of the Competition. Employees and immediate families of Reece Pty Ltd (ABN 84 004 097 090, 118 Burwood Highway, Burwood, Victoria 3125, alternatively by telephone on 03 9274 0000) (Reece), associated companies and agencies are ineligible to enter the Competition.
  3. The Competition commences on 1 October 2013 at 9am AEST and entries close on 8 November 2013 at 5pm AEDT (entry period). The voting period commences on 16 November 2013 at 9am AEDT and will close on 29 November 2013 at 5pm AEDT (voting period). Any entries submitted after the entry period will not be considered.
  4. Entry is online. To enter, participants must complete an entry form, and submit their DIY story (in 200 words or less) on the online entry box along with their name, telephone number and Reece customer account number, and select the ‘submit’ button. Entry forms are available on the Reece website (www.reece.com.au/diydisasters) and in the Competition ‘App’ for iOS. You may be directed to the entry form on the Reece website from the Reece Plumbing Facebook page (facebook.com/ReecePlumbing). Entrants acknowledge that their entry online is only received by the Reece once they have received a ‘confirmation message’.
  5. Entrants may submit up to five photographs with their Competition entry. These photographs will not be returned to the entrant. Each entrant warrants that they are the author of the story that they submit, that they have the authority to submit the photographs for the purpose of the Competition and that they have the right to grant Reece permission to use the story and the photographs as contemplated by these terms and conditions.
  6. One entrant for the first five weeks of the entry period will be selected as a finalist based on the entry’s creativity and originality by the Plumbing Business Development Manager at the Reece Support Centre at 118 Burwood Highway, Burwood, Victoria 3125. Five more finalists will be selected in the final week of the entry period in the same manner.
  7. Finalists are voted for on Facebook by Facebook users. The entry with the most votes during the voting period will be deemed the grand prize winner. Finalists’ entries will also be displayed on Reece’s website.
  8. All Facebook voters that nominate the grand prize winner’s entry will go into the draw to win $1,000 cash (Facebook Voters Competition). The Facebook Voters Competition is governed by separate terms and conditions to these terms and conditions, which may be accessed at www.reece.com.au/diydisasters/facebookterms.
  9. The grand prize is $5,000.00 cash (grand prize). The grand prize winner will be confirmed on 2 December 2013 as voted by Facebook users during the voting period. The winner will be notified on or before 4 December 2013 by their Branch Manager by telephone and their name will be published in the February 2014 edition of the Outlet Newsletter.
  10. The grand prize will be paid to the grand prize winner by crossed cheque. The grand prize will be paid to the grand prize winner before 13 January 2013.
  11. All 10 finalists, including the grand prize winner, will receive a Big Dog finalist prize pack (finalist prize pack). Each finalist prize pack contains:

    BIGDOG POST HOLE F/GLASS HANDLE SHOVEL = $29.95
    BIGDOG TAPE MEASURE - 8M/25MM = $17.95
    BIGDOG PLUMBERS SOCKET SET (15 PCE) = $69.95
    BIGDOG OPEN TOTE TOOL BAG = $57.53
    BIGDOG BOXER (MEDIUM) = $59.99
    BIGDOG HACKSAW NEW = $34.95
    BIGDOG ADJ WRENCHES - 4 Pce Kit = $99.95
    BIGDOG LEVEL (3 VIAL) - 1200MM = $54.95
    BIGDOG CLAW HAMMER 20 OZ = $36.95
    BIGDOG SCREWDRIVER SET-6 PCE = $20.41
    BIGDOG TORPEDO LEVEL - 230MM = $14.19
    BIGDOG WIRE BRUSH = $6.95
    (Total Value $500).

    The value of the finalist prize pack includes GST. The individual prize values listed above are the recommended retail value as at the time of printing. Reece accepts no responsibility for any variation in the price of the individual items in the finalist price pack thereafter. The items in the finalist prize pack are not transferable, exchangeable or redeemable for cash.
  12. The finalist prize pack winners will be notified by their Branch Manager by telephone within two days of being announced as a finalist. Each finalist prize pack will be delivered before 13 January 2013 to the Reece branch nominated by the winner of each prize for collection by that winner.
  13. If the grand prize is unclaimed by 3 March 2014, the entrant (including finalists) with the second most Facebook votes will win the grand prize. If a finalist prize pack is unclaimed by 3 March 2014, the Plumbing Business Development Manager will select an entry (not including finalists) based on the entry’s creativity and originality. This entrant will win the unclaimed finalist prize pack. The unclaimed prize winner(s) will be notified by their Branch Manager by telephone within two days of being selected as an unclaimed prize winner.
  14. All entries become the property of Reece. The entries may be entered into a database for future promotional, marketing and publicity purposes. Each entrant grants Reece the right to reproduce, publish, perform, communicate, adapt or otherwise use stories and photographs used to enter the Competition at any time in the future for any purpose (including commercial purposes) free of any charge.
  15. Reece accepts no responsibility for late, lost, misplaced or misdirected entries.
  16. Except for any liability that cannot be excluded by law, Reece will not be liable for any failure to enter a qualifying person into the Competition where such a failure arises out of any technical difficulties or equipment malfunction (whether or not under the Reece’s control).
  17. Reece is not be liable for any loss or damage whatsoever suffered, including but not limited to direct or consequential loss, or personal injury suffered or sustained in connection with the prizes.
  18. Reece, agencies and companies associated with this Competition will take no responsibility for prizes damaged or lost in transit.
  19. The Competition is in no way sponsored, endorsed or administered by, or associated with, Facebook.
