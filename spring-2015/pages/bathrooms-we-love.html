  • Start with the basics. Make sure you have a set of drawings to scale.

Top plumber tips for renovating a bathroom.

  • Choose fixtures, fittings and tiles that won’t date too quickly
  • A white or light colour scheme will make your bathroom appear more spacious.
  • Don’t go too cheap with the fixtures and fittings or don’t be surprised if they don’t last.
  • A good bathroom renovation done properly will take a few weeks so make sure you’ve made other arrangements if you don’t have a second bathroom.

hear from
the tradies

A successful bathroom renovation starts with smart planning and a good plumber. Ben Kerr of Shore Plumbing worked with CM studios to help bring their design to life. We asked Ben to share his top plumbing tips.

What was the clients brief and overall look they were going for?

A modern, simple and elegant style.

What was the project highlight?

The project highlight would have to be the overall transformation of the existing layout.

What’s your favourite feature of the bathroom?

I really like the wet area. And the pendant light above the basin is pretty cool.

Did existing plumbing compromise the layout?

No, this bathroom was extended out to the hall. The new design was a completely different layout to the existing design, so all new water and drainage was installed.

What are some of the latest trends in bathrooms?

This layout is becoming more common. To extend the bathroom and create an open wet area around the bath and shower.
