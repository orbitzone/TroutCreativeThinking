The Garden Bathroom - Kerrie and Gerard Maggs

A big congratulations to Kerrie Maggs and her husband Gerard, who are the 2013 winners of the Reece ‘I Love My Bathroom’ competition. Complete with an amazing private garden, outdoor bath and rain shower, this really is the ultimate sanctuary.

‘This really is my happy place’

Kerrie Maggs

Two years ago Kerrie Maggs underwent surgery to have a cochlear ear implant, restoring hearing she had lost during a prior operation. There was never a question of where she would spend her recovery. “My home – and especially this bathroom – became my sanctuary,” she says.


A natural connection.

When they first began putting together ideas for their bathroom, Kerrie and Gerard Maggs wanted a space that really connected with nature, so a private courtyard was planned from the start. Their landscaper, Dean Herald, then suggested the idea of adding an outdoor bath - and the incredible result really speaks for itself.


Organically inspired lines.

From the very start, Kerrie wanted clean, simple fixtures and immediately fell in love with the beautiful Laufen freestanding bath and pedestal basins after visiting a Reece showroom. Kerri said that ‘In the square bathroom their rounded features really help to balance the space.’


A very happy place.

This gorgeous bathroom gets plenty of use too. Kerrie went on to say that her husband Gerard uses the outdoor shower all the time in summer. Kerrie on the other hand finds the indoor bathroom very cocooning and calming. ‘At night it’s lovely to light some candles in the courtyard and soak in the indoor bath looking out to the garden. It really is my happy place.’ said Kerrie.
