Reece BIA 2013

Now in its eighth year, the Reece Bathroom Innovation Award (BIA) is one of Australia’s most highly regarded product design competitions. This year, the 2013 BIA has an exciting new focus around the role of technology in the bathroom.

Technology now plays a key role in the home. People are no longer surprised when a product comes with an app to control it and most digital devices can be personalised for our everyday needs. These advancements have enabled us to create highly individual spaces. Exploring the potential of technology in the bathroom offers a unique platform for creating groundbreaking ideas that can positively impact people lives. Find out more about the full competition brief here.

Entries for the Reece BIA 2013 will be open from 2 April 2013, and will close on 28 June 2013 and winners will be announced at Sydney Indesign: THE EXPERIENCE, August 15-17, 2013.

This is a unique opportunity for professional and student product designers to showcase their talent on the world stage and share in prizes to the value of $35,000. So start creating today!
