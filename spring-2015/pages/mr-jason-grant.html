Jason's
Tips

  • Day Spa is about creating your own personal wellness oasis. A place where you can escape the outside world.
  • Favour organically inspired designs to introduce a sense of calm and serenity.
  • Take inspiration from water. Recreate a waterfall like experience with a floor mounted bath mixer or rain shower.
  • Bring the outdoors in with plenty of light. A big garden window or skylight make a great addition.
  • Beautiful framed photography and candles are the perfect way to finish off the bathroom and make the space feel inviting.
