When your bathroom renovation is complete, you can rejoice in its happiness every day. Here we look at how one Australian couple successfully tackled their recent bathroom renovation.

When Kate and Derek embarked on their bathroom renovation in late 2013, their vision was to create a calming space that would showcase the impressive form and geometry of their statement bath. This bedroom turned bathroom needed to be a functional space, generous with light and bold in material choices. The couple knew that the design choices made in this room.
