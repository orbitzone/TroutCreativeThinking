Day Spa Tips

by Lyndall from
Aurora Day Spa

Mood lighting

Lighting really sets the atmosphere. If natural light is available, harness that. If not, candlelight has a soothing, calming effect.

Therapeutic salts

A warm bath with the addition of therapeutic salts can physically relax your body and prepare you for sleep - an ideal finish to the day.
