How To Enter

Entering the Reece Bathroom Innovation Award is easy. Just follow these simple steps and ensure you read the full Rules & Conditions.

Step One

Register here to receive your unique entry code. We’ll keep you updated with news and announcements during the entry period. The unique entry code is used to "tag" your entry submission - ensuring the judging process remains anonymous.

Step Two

Select a category for your product concept from The Brief.

Step Three

Review the Rules & Conditions to ensure you are eligible.

Step Four

Prepare your entry, including 2D & 3D drawings and a typed design statement of no more than 500 words. Entries should be foramtted as high resolution PDF files.

Step Five

Submit your entry online or by post according to the instructions in your entry email. You can request another copy of the instructions here.
