2014 DIY Plumbing Disasters Competition

Terms and Conditions

  1. Information on how to enter and prizes form part of the conditions of entry. Entry into the 2014 DIY Plumbing Disasters Competition shall be deemed an acknowledgement and acceptance of these terms and conditions.

  2. Entry to the Competition is free and only open to Australian residents who are Reece Account Customers during the period of the Competition. Employees and immediate families of Reece Pty Ltd (ABN 84 004 097 090, 118 Burwood Highway, Burwood, Victoria 3125, alternatively by telephone on 03 9274 0000) (Reece), associated companies and agencies are ineligible to enter the Competition.

  3. The Competition commences on 1 October 2014 at 9am AEST and entries close on 14 November 2014 at 5pm AEDT (entry period). Any entries submitted after the entry period will not be considered.

  4. Entry is online. To enter, participants must complete an entry form, and submit their DIY story (in 50 words or less) on the online entry box along with their name, telephone number and Reece customer account number, and select the 'submit' button. Entry forms are available on the Reece website (www.reece.com.au/diydisasters). Entrants acknowledge that their entry online is only received by Reece once they have received a 'confirmation message'.

  5. Entrants are required to submit one photograph with their Competition entry. This photograph will not be returned to the entrant. Each entrant warrants that they are the author of the story that they submit, that they have the authority to submit the photograph for the purpose of the Competition and that they have the right to grant Reece permission to use the story and the photograph as contemplated by these terms and conditions.

  6. The grand prize and runner up prize winners will be determined on 28 November 2014 by Reece's Plumbing Business Development Manager and four industry experts. The entries will be judged on the basis of the entry's originality, humour, the ability to explain how the entrant would fix the DIY disaster or what they would have done differently.

  7. The grand prize is $5,000.00 cash (grand prize). The runner up prize is $1,000.00 cash (runner up prize). The grand prize and runner up winners will be confirmed on 1 December 2014 as decided by a panel of 4 industry experts and Reece's Plumbing Business Development Manager. The grand prize and runner up winners will be notified on or before 4 December 2014 by their Branch Manager by telephone and their name will be published in the February 2015 edition of the Outlet Newsletter. Winning entries will also be displayed on the Promoter's website.  

  8. The second runner up prize is a BigDog prize pack (second runner up prize pack). The runner up prize pack contains a BIGDOG POST HOLE F/GLASS HANDLE SHOVEL = $33.50, BIGDOG TAPE MEASURE - 8M/25MM = $20.24, BIGDOG PLUMBERS SOCKET SET (15 PCE) = $83.38, BIGDOG OPEN TOTE TOOL BAG = $62.26, BIGDOG HEAVY DUTY PRO CAULKING GUN = $33.17, BIGDOG POP RIVET GUN = $37.84, BIGDOG RETRACTABLE KNIFE = $18.87, BIGDOG WORKBROOM 450MM = $31.30, BIGDOG HACKSAW 300MM = $39.77, BIGDOG CLAW HAMMER 20 OZ = $42.08, BIGDOG SCREWDRIVER SET-6 PCE = $22.11, BIGDOG INSPECTION MIRROR KIT = $32.45, BIGDOG LONG ARM HEX KEY SET = $23.76, BIGDOG COLD CHISEL 25MM = $20.96 (Total Value $500). The value of the second runner up prize pack includes GST. The individual prize values listed above are the recommended retail value as at the time of printing. Reece accepts no responsibility for any variation in the price of the individual items in the second runner up prize pack thereafter. The items in the second runner up prize pack are not transferable, exchangeable or redeemable for cash.

  9. The grand prize and runner up prize will be paid to the grand prize and runner up winners by crossed cheque. The grand prize and runner up prize will be paid to the winners before 12 January 2015.

  10. The second runner up prize pack winner will be notified on or before 4 December 2014 by their Branch Manager by telephone and their name will be published in the February 2015 edition of the Outlet Newsletter. The second runner up prize pack will be delivered before 12 January 2015 to the Reece branch nominated by the second runner up winner.  

  11. If the grand prize is unclaimed by 2 March 2015, the runner up winner as selected by the judging panel will win the grand prize. If the runner up prize is unclaimed by 2 March 2015, the second runner up as selected by the judging panel will win the runner up prize. If the second runner up prize is unclaimed by 2 March 2015, the Plumbing Business Development manager will select an entry (not including the grand prize winner and runner up winner) based on the entry’s originality, humour, the ability to explain how the entrant would fix the DIY disaster or what they would have done differently. This entrant will win the unclaimed second runner up prize pack. The unclaimed prize winner will be notified by their Branch Manager by telephone within 2 days of being selected as an unclaimed prize winner.

  12. All entries become the property of Reece. The entries may be entered into a database for future promotional, marketing and publicity purposes.
