Judges

Submissions for the BIA 2013 will be assessed by a panel of industry leaders, who represent all areas of design, production and marketing.

Justin Wagemakers
Blue Sky Design Group

Two time winner of the Reece Bathroom Innovation Award, Justin Wagemakers, joins the 2013 judging panel to help find Australia’s next design success story.

Wagemakers has welcomed new cultures and ideas from a very young age, travelling extensively with his parents, who were diplomats.

Settling in The Netherlands, Wagemakers graduated from The Design Academy, working with Scope Design Strategy and Phillips Design on innovative ventures such as the Noah’s Ark Experience Design project. In 2006, Justin migrated to Australia and now works as a senior designer at Blue Sky Design Group in Sydney.

Piet Billekens
Designer for Nobili

Piet Billekens has been creating beautiful bathroom products since finishing his Industrial Design Degree in his home country, the Netherlands in 1989. He quickly moved to Milan and began an award-winning career designing products for the most important room in the home.

Over the years, Piet has won numerous awards and international competitions with projects related to the bathroom sector. But he has also given back to the industry by guest lecturing young minds at the Milan Polytechnic and the University of Rome La Sapienza.

Today, Piet is the principal of his own company Foresee Design and he has also participated as an expert member working with The European Committee for Standardisation (CEN) in the development of legislation.

Stuart Constantine
Core77 and Coroflot, Co-founder

Stuart Constantine is a co-founder and managing partner of Core77, a New York-based publishing company focusing on the design industries. He currently oversees financial and technical operations for Core77.com, Coroflot.com and DesignDirectory.com as well as various other partner sites.

Stuart has over 15 years experience in the technology industry with particular emphasis on interactive design, industrial design, product development and management.

He studied History at the University of Connecticut and Industrial Design at Pratt Institute. He currently resides in Connecticut with his wife, three young children, a collection of guitars and a sailboat.

Josep Congost
Design Manager of Roca Design Center & Innovation Lab, Barcelona

As Design Manager for Roca, the world’s largest global bathroom products company, Josep Congost oversees a multidisciplinary team that spans the fields of engineering, manufacturing, design and development.

From 1981 to 1989, he was a founding associate and President of the Board of Directors at Ecotècnia S. Coop. And from 1989 to 1997, he served as Director of Vehicle Design for the Nissan European Technology Center of Barcelona (NETC-B).

He also holds a degree in Engineering from the Polytechnic University of Catalonia.

In 1997, Josep began working with Roca Sanitario, SA and today he is the Design Manager at the renowned Roca Design Center & Innovation Lab in Barcelona, Spain.

Shaynna Blaze
Blank Canvas Design

Shaynna Blaze is an Award-Winning Interior Designer who has created stunning interiors in residential homes and commercial spaces for over twenty years. Armed with a vast portfolio of work in various fields of design, Shaynna is also a successful commissioned artist. Shaynna transforms commercial, private and retail premises from the most modest budgets to million dollar projects via her successful Interior Design business, ‘blankcanvas INTERIORS’.

Since 2008, Shaynna’s talents were brought to a broader audience through her role as Interior Designer and Co-Presenter on the The LifeStyle Channel’s Award Winning Show, Selling Houses Australia. It was here that Shaynna was able to showcase her design skills, along with her warm and approachable presenting style, which have consequently led to regular appearances as a design expert across a number of mainstream shows including The Morning Show, The Circle, Today Tonight, A Current Affair and more.

In 2012 Shaynna was chosen to be the 3rd expert judge on the successful series on Channel 9: ‘The Block’ (alongside John McGrath and Neale Whitaker). Shaynna also launched her range of flameless wax candles in conjunction with Enjoy Lighting® Australia and this year launched her book ‘Design Your Home’.

Greg Natale
Greg Natale Design

Since 2001, Greg Natale Design has pioneered the integration of design and decoration; focusing on residential, retail and commercial projects. With a bold signature style, Greg’s work is instantly recognisable. Greg Natale Design has won numerous awards and appeared in range of local and international design publications. At the 2011 Belle Coco Republic Interior Design Awards, Greg won the award for Interior Designer of the Year.

Since opening Greg Natale Design, the business has expanded into residential architecture, with The Tierney Watson House shortlisted in the DIA Interior Design Awards and Inside Magazine Idea Awards - gracing the covers of both Belle Magazine and Inside Magazine concurrently.

In 2009 Greg Natale Design also collaborated with Designer Rugs to release a new range for both commercial and domestic use, realising the firm’s objective of diversifying across multiple design disciplines. The integration of architecture, design and decoration is the firm’s primary motivation and design philosophy.

Owen Lynch
Editor of Indesign Live

Owen Lynch is currently Editor of Indesignlive.com and writer for Indesign Magazine at the Indesign Group: a cross-media publishing company responsible for Indesign, Habitus, DQ, The Collection, Binder, indesignlive.com, habitusliving.com and a series of international industry events.

Holding an Industrial Design degree from UTS, Owen’s career to date has spanned design, consultancy and media. Owen has worked across a range of projects for clients including CLOUSTON associates, Stylecraft, KE-ZU, LiTEsource, AHL and The Authentic Design Alliance.
